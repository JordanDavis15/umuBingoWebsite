Frequently Asked Questions

A SOL wallet is an address that allows SOL or SOL tokens to be sent and received. These wallets often come in the form of software that is easily installed on any mobile device. After installing a SOL wallet, input the given address into the login screen to play today's game.

Desktop:

  • Phantom Wallet
  • Solflare Wallet
Android:
  • Phantom Wallet
  • Solflare Wallet
IOS:
  • Phantom Wallet
  • Solflare Wallet

After entering your SOL address and choosing a category of questions, a bingo card is presented to you. There is a question displayed at the top of the screen which you should try to answer by choosing a square on the bingo card. If the answer is not on the board, click or press (on mobile) the question and it will display a new question. If you choose the correct answer, a X will display in that board space. If you choose the incorrect answer, nothing will happen to the board - guess again!

Click or press (on mobile) the question to see a new question.

A player wins by getting a complete row or column marked with an X.

You cannot close the tab and return to your game. If you start a game and then close your tab, you cannot play again that day. If you step away without closing your tab, your time does not pause. When you close the app on mobile, you cannot resume your game if you had not completed it.

A player can only play 1 game per wallet per day.

There is only 1 winner per day. There are not separate winners for each category.

Enter your SOL address into the login page and click login. This will display the daily scoreboard if you have already played. If you have not already played, you must complete your daily game before being redirected to the scoreboard.

You will receive your UMU Coin(s) shortly after midnight on the day you won.
