Freqently Asked Questions

A SOL wallet is an address that allows SOL or SOL tokens to be sent and received. These wallets often come in the form of software that is easily installed on any mobile device. After installing a SOL wallet, input the given address into the login screen to play today's game.

After entering your SOL address and choosing a category of questions, a bingo card is presented to you. There is a question is displayed at the top of the screen which you should try to answer by choosing a square on the bingo card. If the answer is not on the board, click or press (on mobile) the question and it will display a new question.
